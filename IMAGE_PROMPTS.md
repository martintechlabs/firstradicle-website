# Image Assets & Prompts

This document lists all custom-generated images used in the First Radicle website, their file locations, usage context, and the prompts used (or intended) to generate them.

**Style Guide for All Images:**
*   **Style:** Minimalist technical line art, architectural blueprints, organic growth metaphors.
*   **Colors:** Forest green, cream/beige background, subtle gold accents.
*   **Vibe:** Professional, engineering-focused, "anti-hype", structured but organic.

---

## 1. Hero & Brand

### `hero-growth.png`
*   **Location:** `client/public/images/hero-growth.png`
*   **Usage:** Homepage Hero Section (Main visual)
*   **Prompt:** "A minimalist technical illustration of a mechanical tree growing out of a circuit board root system. The roots are complex digital pathways, the trunk is structured engineering, and the leaves are subtle data nodes. Cream background, forest green lines, high precision, architectural style."

### `hero-architecture.png`
*   **Location:** `client/public/images/hero-architecture.png`
*   **Usage:** Unblock Session Page / Secondary Hero
*   **Prompt:** "Isometric blueprint of a clean, scalable software architecture. Server nodes, database cylinders, and load balancers connected by neat, organized lines. No chaos. Professional engineering diagram style. Forest green and gold on cream."

### `logo.png`
*   **Location:** `client/public/images/logo.png`
*   **Usage:** Navbar, Footer
*   **Description:** First Radicle brand logo (Leaf/Tech hybrid icon).

## 2. The Problem (Pain Points)

### `pain-tangle.png`
*   **Location:** `client/public/images/pain-tangle.png`
*   **Usage:** Homepage "The Problem" Section (Visualizing "Spaghetti Code")
*   **Prompt:** "Abstract line art of a tangled, chaotic knot of wires and cables. Represents technical debt and messy no-code logic. The knot is tight and confusing. Dark green lines on cream background. Minimalist but messy."

### `pain-chaos.png`
*   **Location:** `client/public/images/pain-chaos.png`
*   **Usage:** Homepage "The Problem" Section (Visualizing "Scaling Walls")
*   **Prompt:** "A simple illustration of a brick wall blocking a path. The path is digital/circuit-like and hits a dead end at the wall. Represents hitting the limits of no-code tools. Architectural sketch style."

## 3. The Solution & Process

*   **Location:** `client/public/images/solution-order.png`
*   **Usage:** Homepage "The Solution" Section
*   **Prompt:** "A perfectly organized server rack or database schema diagram. Symmetrical, clean, balanced. Represents the stability of code. Fine line art, forest green."

*   **Usage:** Homepage "Our Process" Section (Step 1: Audit)

### `process-flow.png`
*   **Location:** `client/public/images/process-flow.png`
*   **Usage:** Homepage "Our Process" Section (Step 2: Migration)
